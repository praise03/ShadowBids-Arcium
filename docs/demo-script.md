# ShadowBid 3-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00 - Problem

Open auctions leak demand. Every bidder can see or infer competitive pressure, which changes behavior before the market closes.

## 0:30 - Product

ShadowBid lets a creator open a sealed-bid auction on Solana. Bidders submit encrypted bid payloads, and the chain only stores commitments while the auction is live.

## 1:10 - Flow

Show the auction board, open the validator seat auction, connect wallet, and submit a sealed bid. Highlight that the bid amount is not shown in public auction state.

## 1:50 - Finalization

Close bidding, trigger confidential compute, and show the final result card. Only winner and winning amount are revealed.

## 2:30 - Why Arcium

Arcium gives the project a credible private compute path: bids can be compared without exposing all participant inputs.

## 2:50 - Closing

ShadowBid turns sealed-bid auctions into a reusable primitive for allocations, launch slots, services, and private capital formation on Solana.
